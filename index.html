Social Seat

Social Seat is a product we have designed to measure the social temperature of the University of Queensland campus. We've designed a special automatic fold-down chair which can gage the level of sociality in a classroom environment. The chair has also features a small touch-screen on its arm. These chairs will soon fill classrooms campus-wide!
